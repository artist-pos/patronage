# Patronage — Product Marketing Context

---

## Product

**Name**: Patronage
**URL**: patronage.nz
**Positioning**: Infrastructure for artists. The place creatives check for opportunities.
**Description**: Free professional infrastructure for NZ and Australian creatives across all disciplines. A weekly-updated directory of grants, residencies, open calls, commissions, prizes and jobs — plus professional profiles used for grant applications and professional introductions.

**What Patronage is not**: A social media platform. An art marketplace (first). An art magazine.

---

## Market & Geography

- **Primary**: New Zealand (Aotearoa)
- **Secondary**: Australia
- **Trans-Tasman**: NZ and AUS in one place — a key differentiator
- **Language**: NZ/British spelling. "Kia ora" is appropriate.

---

## Target Audiences

### 1. Creatives
- Artists, musicians, writers, poets, dancers, filmmakers
- Build a professional profile, discover and apply for opportunities
- Profile at patronage.nz/username — used for grant applications and professional introductions

### 2. Patrons
- Collectors, supporters, job seekers
- Follow artists, collect works, apply for jobs

### 3. Partners (B2B)
- Galleries, councils, organisations
- List opportunities, manage applications via pipeline

---

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Opportunity directory** | Weekly-updated grants, residencies, open calls, commissions, prizes, jobs — NZ and AUS |
| **Professional profiles** | Portfolio, CV, exhibition history, studio updates, bibliography at patronage.nz/username |
| **Provenance system** | Artwork transfer certificates with public verification URLs |
| **Application pipeline** | Partner organisations run open calls; artists apply directly; partners manage via dashboard |
| **Studio Feed** | Live project updates — image, audio, video, text, embed |
| **Work marketplace** | List available works, pricing, collection management |
| **Weekly digest** | New + closing-soon opportunities by email every week |

---

## Key Differentiators

- **Curated and verified** — every opportunity reviewed before publishing
- **Cross-discipline** — visual art, music, writing, poetry, dance, film
- **Trans-Tasman** — NZ and AUS in one place
- **Provenance system** — artwork transfer certificates, public verification URLs
- **Free for creatives** — always

---

## Stage

Early stage. Live platform. First institutional partnership with Elam School of Fine Arts (University of Auckland). Expanding to music, writing, dance, and film disciplines. Building user base across NZ and AUS creative institutions.

---

## Pricing

- **Free for creatives** — always
- Partners: managed submission service (pricing TBC)
- Planned: commission on artwork transfers post-traction (not yet in user-facing copy)

## Tone of Voice

**Be**: Clear. Practical. Supportive. Professional but informal.
**Kia ora** is appropriate.

**Never**: Corporate, academic, or pretentious. Long sentences. Jargon. Marketing clichés.

**Banned phrases**: "discursive", "liminal", "dialogic", "unlock your potential", "empowering creatives", "redefining the ecosystem"
