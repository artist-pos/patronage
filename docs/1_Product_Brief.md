\### STICKY RULES FOR CLAUDE

1\. Always prioritize mobile-first design.

2\. Use the "Institutional Minimalist" aesthetic (No gradients, black/white).

3\. Do NOT suggest features outside of v1.0 scope.



1. Vision \& Purpose
   Patronage is a clean, highly-utilitarian digital infrastructure for artists in Aotearoa (NZ) and Australia. It reduces friction in funding discovery and artist identity management, providing a reliable pipeline for artists and a verified directory for institutional funders.

   The Philosophy: Infrastructure, not an ideology. Professional, not social.

   The Constraint: Mobile-first design is strictly required.

2. Core Value Propositions

   For Artists: A "TradeMe-simple" engine to find money and a professional vanity URL to prove their history.

   For Partners: A centralized hub to list opportunities and a managed gateway to discover talent.

3. Intellectual Property
   Artists retain full IP of all uploaded works. Patronage holds a display license only for the purposes of the directory.
